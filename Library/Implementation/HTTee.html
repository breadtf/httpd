Tee stream

This is part of libwww . The Tee stream just writes everything you put into it into two oter streams.

One use (the only use?!) is for taking a cached copey on disk while loading the main copy, without having to wait for the disk copy to be finished and reread it. 

#include "HTStream.h"

extern HTStream * HTTee PARAMS((HTStream* s1, HTStream* s2));
